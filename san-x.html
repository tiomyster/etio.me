Wrestlers are 16 times more likely to contract skin infections than other athletes because it is difficult to clean mats effectively. Current mop and bucket methods are don't get the job done.

I collaborated with a team of 18 to design and prototype an athletic mat cleaner and sterilizer. Our product, San-X, is an industrial sized machine that combines steam cleaning with UV radiation to remove ringworm and other dangerous skin diseases.We created subteams to ensure our product was functional, safe, and easy to use.

Defining Product Contract - Translated user needs into functional requirements.

Cleaning Subsystem - Designed a mechanism that allowed user to easily replace the machine washable cleaning pad.

Working in Parallel - Communicated with other subteams to integrate our subsystems in a timely manner.

Presentation Graphics - Created animations and graphics for our final presentation.
