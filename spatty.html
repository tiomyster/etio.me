CARNIVAL ROBOT

MIT Spring 2012

2.007 - Design for Manufacturing It

summary

Design and Manufacturing is a mechanical design class and robot competition at MIT. I designed and built a remote controlled robot that flings the slug to hit the bell of a carnival game. For this project I brainstormed and prototyped many mechanisms to accomplish this task. In the end I settled on a flinging mechanism consisting of many torsion springs, a spatula, and a motor-controlled pin to lock it in position. A motor-controlled paddle was used to push the pedal down so the robot could easily slide under the slug.
