YO-YO

MIT Fall 2012

2.008 - Design for Manufacturing II

overview

For this project, I worked with an 8 person team to design a press-fit yo-yo meant to be mass produced using injection molding and thermoforming processes. The molds for the parts were fabricated using a CNC mill. I was responsible for designing the needle of the record. The mold was strategically designed to maximize the number of parts created at once and hide the indications of an injection molded part.
