TECHFAIR MARKETING

MIT Fall 2012

MIT Techfair

overview

Techfair is a student organization dedicated to changing the way students and companies interact at career fairs. Each year we hold a technology expo that features companies, start-ups, and student projects. As part of the marketing team for Techfair, I was responsible for designing and fabricating giant letters to increase campus awareness of our event. These letters were designed to have letters on each side of the block, replicating children's alphabet blocks and encouraging students to reorganize the letters.
