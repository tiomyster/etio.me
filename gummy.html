GUMMY WORM MATERIAL PROPERTIES

MIT Spring 2013

2.671 - Measurement and Instrumentation

summary

For this class, I was required to make measurements on on something of personal interest. As a lover of gummy worms, I decided to measure the elasticity of gummy worms. I used an Instron machine to steadily increase a load applied to the gummy worm. By observing the change in strain, I was able to calculate Young's Modulus for each gummy worm. While performing my experiments, I noticed that the gummy worms showed signs of viscoelastic behavior, so I adjusted my experimental procedure to measure each gummy worm's equilibrium relaxation modulus.
