ONE HAND WASHER

MIT Spring 2013

D-Lab Design

overview

The One Hand Washer is a product created for Emmanuela, a 5 year old amputee from Ghana. Laundry in Ghana is typicallydone using a 2 hand washing method. This device features a plunger with holes punched out mounted on a lever, making washing clothes easy for Emmanuela. This project taught me about appropriate technology, problem framing, and how to empathize with the user.
