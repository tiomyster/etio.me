TECHNOTURTLE

MIT Spring 2012

2.00B - Toy Design

overview

Technoturtle is a spinning music synthesizer that transforms a tone matrix into a tangible experience. The project began as an ordinary music synthesizer, but through extensive prototyping and user testing, our project evolved into a fun, interactive toy. I was in charge of designing and fabricating the structural housing for the toy. We thermoformed the shell, cast the silicone buttons, and laser cut the stand and button frame. An Arduino Mega was used to control the lights and sound.
