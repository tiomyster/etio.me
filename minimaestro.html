my role

Kids have a hard time staying motivated when learning to play musical instruments, while teachers don't have a good method to monitor their students' progress.

I worked with a team of 3 to create an organizational tool for music teachers and students that provides a framework for teachers and students to organize their lessons, manage homework, and track progress.

Minimaestro will focus around recordings uploaded to the site by students, which can later be reviewed by teachers or the students themselves.

Interviewing - Interviewed young music students and music teachers to understand user goals and create personas to design for.

Storyboarding - Creating potential scenarios and sketching preliminary designs for user interfaces.

User Testing - Created low fidelity prototypes and designed tasks for testing. Both conducted and observed user testing.

Implementation - Used JavaScript to create a snake game that incentivizes users to upload more recordings.

Collaborators: Vineet Gopal, Annie Tang, Devin Zhang
